Overview

DailyFeels ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • If you're signed into iCloud, this data syncs to your personal iCloud account.
  • We do not have access to your mood data â€” it remains entirely under your control.

Advertising Data

  • We use Google AdMob to display advertisements.

How We Use Your Information

  • Mood Data: Used solely to display your mood history and statistics within the app.
  • Advertising Data: Used by Google AdMob to serve relevant advertisements.

Data Storage

  • All mood data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework.
  • If iCloud is enabled, data syncs via Apple's CloudKit to your personal iCloud account.
  • We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

  • We use Google AdMob to display advertisements.
  • AdMob's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device's privacy settings.

Apple iCloud

  • If enabled, your data syncs via iCloud.
  • Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

  1. Open DailyFeels
